How to unsubscribe from Coursera

Canceling your Coursera subscription is straightforward and can be done through their website or mobile app. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process.

CANCEL COURSERA SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your Coursera subscription via the website:

  • Log in to your Coursera account on the Coursera website.
  • Go to 'My Purchases' or 'Manage Subscriptions' in your account settings.
  • Under the 'Subscriptions' tab, find the subscription you wish to cancel (e.g., Coursera Plus).
  • Click the 'Cancel Subscription' option next to the subscription you want to cancel.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the cancellation.

CANCEL COURSERA SUBSCRIPTION VIA MOBILE APP

To cancel your Coursera subscription via the mobile app:

  • Log in to your Coursera account on the Coursera mobile app.
  • Tap the settings gear icon, then tap 'My Subscriptions'.
  • Under the 'Subscriptions' tab, find the subscription you wish to cancel (e.g., Coursera Plus).
  • Tap the 'Cancel Subscription' option next to the subscription you want to cancel.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the cancellation.
