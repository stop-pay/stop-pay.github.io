How to unsubscribe from Max

Learn how to cancel your Max (formerly HBO Max) subscription. Follow these steps to unsubscribe whether you signed up directly through Max or via a third-party provider like Apple, Google, or Amazon.

CANCEL MAX SUBSCRIPTION VIA MAX WEBSITE/APP

If you subscribed directly through Max:

  • Go to the Max website or app and sign in to your account.
  • Go to your Profile or Account section.
  • Select Subscription or Billing.
  • Choose Cancel Subscription and confirm your cancellation.

CANCEL MAX SUBSCRIPTION VIA THIRD-PARTY PROVIDER

If you subscribed through a third-party provider:

  • Determine where you subscribed to Max (directly through Max or a third-party provider).
  • Cancel your subscription through that provider's platform.

CANCEL MAX SUBSCRIPTION VIA APPLE APP STORE

To cancel via the Apple App Store:

  • Go to your iPhone/iPad settings.
  • Tap your Apple ID.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Tap Max.
  • Tap Cancel Subscription.

CANCEL MAX SUBSCRIPTION VIA GOOGLE PLAY STORE

To cancel via the Google Play Store:

  • Open the Play Store.
  • Tap your profile icon.
  • Tap Payments & subscriptions.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Tap Max.
  • Tap Cancel subscription.
