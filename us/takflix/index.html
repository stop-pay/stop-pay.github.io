How to unsubscribe from Takflix

Takflix offers individual movie tickets and Tovarystvo memberships. To cancel your Takflix account, follow these steps:

DELETE ACCOUNT VIA TAKFLIX PLATFORM

  • Go to the "My Cinema" section on the Takflix platform.
  • Select "Settings".
  • Edit your personal profile data or change your password.
  • Click "Block profile" to delete your account.

CANCEL GOOGLE PLAY SUBSCRIPTION

  • To manage a Google Play subscription, follow the next steps.
  • On your Android device, go to subscriptions in Google Play.
  • Select the Takflix subscription you want to cancel.
  • Tap cancel subscription and follow the instructions.
