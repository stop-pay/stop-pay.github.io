How to unsubscribe from Work.ua

Work.ua is Ukraine's largest service for finding jobs and employees. To manage or cancel your Work.ua subscription, follow these general steps. Since specific cancellation instructions are not readily available, you may need to contact their customer support for assistance.

UNSUBSCRIBE VIA WORK.UA WEBSITE

Here's how to cancel your Work.ua subscription through their website:

  • Go to the Work.ua official website: https://www.work.ua.
  • Log in to your Work.ua account using your credentials.
  • Navigate to your account settings or profile section.
  • Look for a 'Subscription' or 'Billing' option within your account settings.
  • Review your current subscription plan details.
  • If a cancellation option is available, follow the on-screen instructions to cancel your subscription.
  • If you cannot find a cancellation option, contact Work.ua's customer support directly for assistance.
  • Check your email for a confirmation of your cancellation.
