How to unsubscribe from BarkBox

Learn how to cancel your BarkBox subscription through their website or by contacting customer service. Follow these steps to disable auto-renewal and avoid future charges.

CANCEL VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your BarkBox subscription via the website:

  • Go to the BarkBox website and log in to your account.
  • Navigate to 'Subscription' or 'Subscription Settings', usually found in the upper right corner.
  • Click on 'All Subscriptions'.
  • Select 'Edit' next to the subscription you wish to cancel.
  • Click 'Disable Subscription Renewal' to stop auto-renewal.
  • Make sure to update your subscription information before the 1st of the upcoming month to ensure the changes apply for the next month.

CANCEL VIA EMAIL

To cancel your BarkBox subscription via email:

  • Email BarkBox customer support at happy@barkbox.com with your account number and cancellation request.

CANCEL VIA LIVE CHAT OR PHONE

To cancel your BarkBox subscription via live chat or phone:

  • Contact them via live chat on their website or call them at 1-855-520-BARK (2275).
