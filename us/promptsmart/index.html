How to unsubscribe from PromptSmart

To cancel your PromptSmart subscription, you'll need to manage it through your Google Play account, as PromptSmart cannot directly cancel it for you. Here's how:

UNSUBSCRIBE VIA GOOGLE PLAY STORE (ANDROID)

To cancel your PromptSmart subscription on an Android device:

  • Open the Google Play Store app on your Android device.
  • Tap on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  • Go to 'Payments & subscriptions'.
  • Select 'Subscriptions'.
  • Find and select 'PromptSmart' from the list of your subscriptions.
  • Tap 'Cancel subscription'.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the cancellation. Your subscription will be terminated at the end of the current billing cycle.
