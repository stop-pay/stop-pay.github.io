How to unsubscribe from Peacock

You can cancel your Peacock subscription online through their website or through your third-party provider (e.g., Apple, Amazon, Roku, Xfinity). Canceling the subscription will stop future payments. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.

CANCEL PEACOCK SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your Peacock subscription via their website:

  • Go to peacocktv.com and sign in to your account.
  • Select your profile icon in the top right corner.
  • Click on Account.
  • Navigate to Plans & Payments and select Change or Cancel Plan.
  • Select Cancel Plan and confirm your cancellation.

CANCEL PEACOCK SUBSCRIPTION VIA APPLE/IOS

To cancel your Peacock subscription via Apple/iOS:

  • Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.

CANCEL PEACOCK SUBSCRIPTION VIA AMAZON/FIRE TV

To cancel your Peacock subscription via Amazon/Fire TV:

  • Go to Apps > Appstore > Subscriptions.

CANCEL PEACOCK SUBSCRIPTION VIA ROKU

To cancel your Peacock subscription via Roku:

  • Go to the Home screen.
  • Select the Peacock channel.
  • Press the
  • button on your remote.
  • Select Manage Subscription.
  • Turn off auto-renew.
