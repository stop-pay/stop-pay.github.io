How to unsubscribe from Grammarly

Here's how to cancel your Grammarly subscription, depending on how you subscribed (website, Google Play Store, or Apple App Store). Cancelling stops automatic renewal, but you'll have access to paid features until the end of your current subscription period. To avoid being charged, cancel at least one day before your renewal date.

CANCEL GRAMMARLY SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your Grammarly subscription via the website:

  • Go to account.grammarly.com and log in to your Grammarly account.
  • Click on "Account" (profile icon or settings gear).
  • Select "Subscription".
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Cancel Subscription".
  • Confirm by clicking "Continue".
  • Choose a reason for cancellation and click "Cancel Subscription" again.

CANCEL GRAMMARLY SUBSCRIPTION VIA GOOGLE PLAY STORE

To cancel your Grammarly subscription via the Google Play Store:

  • Open the Google Play Store app.
  • Tap your profile icon.
  • Go to "Payments & subscriptions" and then "Subscriptions".
  • Select Grammarly.
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription" and confirm.

CANCEL GRAMMARLY SUBSCRIPTION VIA IOS APP STORE

To cancel your Grammarly subscription via the iOS App Store:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  • Tap your name at the top of the Settings menu.
  • Tap "Subscriptions".
  • Find Grammarly in the list and tap "Cancel Subscription".
  • Confirm.
