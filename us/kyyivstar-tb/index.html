How to Unsubscribe from Kyivstar TV

This guide provides several methods to cancel your Kyivstar TV subscription as of February 22, 2026. Choose the method that is most convenient for you.

UNSUBSCRIBE VIA MY KYIVSTAR WEB PORTAL/APP

To cancel your Kyivstar TV subscription via the web portal or app:

  • Go to the My Kyivstar web portal (account.kyivstar.ua) or download the 'My Kyivstar' app from the App Store or Google Play.
  • Log in using your phone number.
  • In the menu, select the 'Services' section.
  • Find 'Kyivstar TV' in the list of connected services.
  • Click 'Manage service' or 'Disable'.
  • Confirm the action. You will receive a notification confirming the service is deactivated.

UNSUBSCRIBE VIA PHONE (CUSTOMER SUPPORT)

To cancel your Kyivstar TV subscription by contacting customer support:

  • Call Customer Support at 466 (free within the Kyivstar network) or 0 800 300 466 (from other networks).
  • Verify your identity by providing the necessary information to the consultant.
  • Request the cancellation of the Kyivstar TV service.
  • The consultant will clarify the details and confirm the service deactivation.
