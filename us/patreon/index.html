How to unsubscribe from Patreon

Patreon allows creators to get paid for their work by providing tools to run a subscription content service. You can cancel your Patreon membership either through the website or the mobile app.

CANCEL PATREON MEMBERSHIP VIA WEBSITE

  • Go to the Patreon website and log in to your account.
  • Click on your profile photo and select "Settings".
  • Click on the "Memberships" tab.
  • Locate the creator you wish to cancel your membership with and click "View details" or "Edit".
  • Select "Cancel your payment" and confirm the cancellation.

CANCEL PATREON MEMBERSHIP VIA MOBILE APP

  • Open the Patreon app and switch to your member profile.
  • Find the creator at the top of the home screen and tap on the desired creator.
  • Tap the three dots in the upper-right corner and select "Your membership".
  • Choose "Edit membership," then tap "Cancel membership".
  • Scroll down and confirm the cancellation.
  • Manage your free membership option and tap "Save and Continue".

CANCEL PATREON MEMBERSHIP VIA GOOGLE PLAY STORE

  • Open the Google Play Store.
  • Tap your profile icon.
  • Select 'Payments & subscriptions,' then 'Subscriptions.'
  • Cancel your Patreon subscription.

CANCEL PATREON MEMBERSHIP VIA APPLE APP STORE

  • Open the Settings app.
  • Tap your name.
  • Select 'Subscriptions.'
  • Cancel your Patreon subscription.
