How to unsubscribe from Chewy Autoship

Chewy Autoship is a subscription service that allows you to schedule regular deliveries of pet supplies. You can cancel your Chewy Autoship subscription at any time without cancellation fees. Here's how:

CANCEL CHEWY AUTOSHIP VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your Chewy Autoship subscription via the website:

  • Log in to your Chewy account on chewy.com.
  • Click on "Manage Autoship".
  • Select the Autoship plan you wish to cancel.
  • Click on "Cancel This Autoship" at the bottom of the page.
  • Cancel at least 24 hours before your next scheduled shipment.

CANCEL CHEWY AUTOSHIP VIA PHONE

To cancel your Chewy Autoship subscription via phone:

  • Call Chewy customer service at 1-800-672-4399.
  • Inform the customer service representative that you want to cancel your Autoship subscription.
  • Confirm the cancellation to avoid future charges.
