How to unsubscribe from Adobe Firefly

Here's how to cancel your Adobe Firefly subscription. To manage your Adobe account, you can go to the Adobe website and sign in to your account. Then, go to Manage Plan and select the plan you want to cancel. Select 'Cancel your plan' and follow the prompts.

CANCEL ADOBE FIREFLY SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your Adobe Firefly subscription via the website, follow these steps:

  • Go to the Adobe website.
  • Log in to your account using your Adobe ID.
  • Locate the section related to subscriptions in your account settings.
  • Review your current plan details.
  • Click on the option to 'Cancel Subscription' or similar.
  • Provide feedback for cancelling (optional).
  • Confirm your decision to cancel.
  • Check for a confirmation message or email from Adobe to ensure successful cancellation.
