How to unsubscribe from Promova

Learn how to cancel your Promova subscription, whether you signed up through their website, the Apple App Store, or Google Play Store. Follow these steps to ensure your subscription is properly cancelled and you avoid future charges.

CANCEL PROMOVA SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your Promova subscription via the website:

  • Log in to your Promova account on the website.
  • Go to 'My profile' and find 'Payments & subscriptions'.
  • Locate your subscription and click the three dots.
  • Choose 'Cancel subscription'.
  • Complete the questionnaire to confirm cancellation.

CANCEL PROMOVA SUBSCRIPTION VIA APPLE APP STORE

To cancel your Promova subscription via the Apple App Store:

  • Open your Apple ID account settings.
  • Go to 'Subscriptions'.
  • Locate the Promova subscription.
  • Cancel the subscription.

CANCEL PROMOVA SUBSCRIPTION VIA GOOGLE PLAY

To cancel your Promova subscription via Google Play:

  • Go to play.google.com.
  • Sign in to your Google account.
  • Locate the Promova subscription.
  • Cancel the subscription.
