How to Unsubscribe from Megogo

Learn how to cancel your Megogo subscription on web, Android, or iOS. Follow these simple steps to manage your subscription and avoid future charges.

UNSUBSCRIBE VIA MEGOGO WEBSITE

To cancel your Megogo subscription via the website:

  • Log in to your Megogo account on the Megogo website.
  • Click on your avatar or profile icon in the top right corner.
  • Navigate to the 'Subscriptions' or 'Services' tab in your account settings.
  • Find your active Megogo plan and click 'Manage' or 'Unsubscribe'.
  • Confirm the cancellation. You might be offered a deal to stay, but proceed to confirm the cancellation until you see a success message.

UNSUBSCRIBE VIA GOOGLE PLAY STORE (ANDROID)

To cancel your Megogo subscription via the Google Play Store:

  • Open the Google Play Store app on your Android device.
  • Tap your profile icon in the top right corner.
  • Select 'Payments & subscriptions' from the menu, then tap 'Subscriptions'.
  • Find Megogo in the list, tap it, and select 'Cancel subscription'.
  • Follow the prompts to confirm.

UNSUBSCRIBE VIA APPLE APP STORE (IOS)

To cancel your Megogo subscription via the Apple App Store:

  • Go to Settings on your iOS device, tap on your Apple ID, then select 'Subscriptions'.
  • Manage your Megogo subscription through the Apple App Store subscription management.
