How to unsubscribe from DeepL Pro

To cancel your DeepL Pro subscription, follow these steps. Make sure to cancel at least one day before your next billing date to avoid being charged. Cancellation is effective at the end of the current billing cycle, and no pro-rata credit is offered for early cancellation.

CANCEL DEEPL PRO SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEB

To cancel your DeepL Pro subscription via the web, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your DeepL account on the DeepL website.
  • Go to 'Subscription' in your account settings.
  • Click 'Terminate subscription'.
  • Cancel at least one day before your next billing date.
  • Confirm the termination.
  • Check the status of your subscription under 'Subscription' to ensure it shows 'Canceled'.

CANCEL DEEPL PRO SUBSCRIPTION VIA APPLE APP STORE (IOS)

To cancel your DeepL Pro subscription purchased through the Apple App Store (iOS), follow these steps:

  • Manage and cancel it through your Apple ID subscription settings.

CANCEL DEEPL PRO SUBSCRIPTION VIA GOOGLE PLAY (ANDROID)

To cancel your DeepL Pro subscription purchased through Google Play (Android), follow these steps:

  • Manage and cancel it through Google Play Subscriptions on your device.
