How to unsubscribe from Microsoft 365

Learn how to cancel your Microsoft 365 subscription and manage your account. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth cancellation process and understand the implications of ending your subscription.

CANCEL MICROSOFT 365 SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEBSITE

To cancel your Microsoft 365 subscription via the website, follow these steps:

  • Go to the Microsoft Subscriptions page at account.microsoft.com/services/ and sign in to the Microsoft account you used to purchase the subscription.
  • Find your Microsoft 365 subscription.
  • Select "Manage".
  • On the next page, select "Cancel" (or it might say "Upgrade or Cancel," depending on your subscription type).
  • Follow the instructions to cancel your subscription.
  • Note that cancellations are effective immediately, even if you're billed monthly or annually.
  • Be aware that canceling your subscription will result in a loss of access to Microsoft applications and data stored in Outlook or OneDrive.

If you purchased your subscription through a third-party provider, follow these steps:
