How to unsubscribe from Netflix: step-by-step instructions

Learn how to quickly cancel your Megogo subscription and stop debiting your card.

  1. Open the megogo.net website and log in to your profile.
  2. Click on the profile icon in the upper right corner.
  3. Go to the 'Subscriptions' section.
  4. Find your active subscription and click the 'Manage' button.
  5. Select 'Disable automatic renewal' or 'Cancel subscription'.
  6. Confirm the action in the window that appears.
