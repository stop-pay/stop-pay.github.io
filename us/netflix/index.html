How to unsubscribe from Netflix

Here's how to cancel your Netflix subscription. After canceling, you can still use Netflix until the end of your current billing period. As of February 2026, Netflix monthly plan prices are: Standard with Ads: $7.99, Standard: $17.99, Premium: $24.99.

CANCEL NETFLIX SUBSCRIPTION VIA WEB BROWSER

To cancel your Netflix subscription via a web browser, follow these steps:

  • Go to your Account page by going to netflix.com/account on a web browser.
  • Sign in if prompted.
  • Click the dropdown menu in the top right corner next to your profile name and click Account.
  • Click "Membership & Billing".
  • Click "Cancel Membership".
  • Click "Finish Cancellation" to confirm.
