How to unsubscribe from ESPN+

There are several ways to cancel your ESPN+ subscription, depending on how you signed up. Here's a comprehensive guide to help you through the process.

CANCEL ESPN+ SUBSCRIPTION ONLINE

Here's how to cancel your ESPN+ subscription via the web:

  • Go to the ESPN accounts page in a web browser and log in.
  • Look for a "Manage" option, then click "Cancel Subscription".
  • Confirm your cancellation.
  • Go to your account page on the Roku website.
  • Click "Manage your subscriptions," then your ESPN+ subscription, and then "Unsubscribe".
  • Go to Amazon's subscriptions page, find ESPN+, select "Actions", and then "Cancel your subscription."

CANCEL ESPN+ SUBSCRIPTION BY PHONE

To cancel your ESPN+ subscription by phone:

  • Call 1-888-549-3776 to speak with a customer support representative.

CANCEL ESPN+ SUBSCRIPTION BY EMAIL

To cancel your ESPN+ subscription by email:

  • Email support@espnplus.com with your full name, subscription details, and cancellation request.

CANCEL ESPN+ SUBSCRIPTION VIA MOBILE APP

Here's how to cancel your ESPN+ subscription via mobile app stores:

  • Tap your Apple ID at the top, then "Subscriptions".
  • Select ESPN+ and "Cancel Subscription".
  • Open the Google Play Store app.
  • Tap your profile icon, then "Payments & Subscriptions" and then "Subscriptions".
  • Choose ESPN+ and "Cancel Subscription".
