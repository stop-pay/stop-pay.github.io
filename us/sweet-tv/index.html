How to unsubscribe from SWEET.TV

Learn how to cancel your SWEET.TV subscription. The process varies depending on how you signed up (website, Google Play, or Apple App Store). SWEET.TV subscriptions are commitment-free and can be cancelled at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle.

CANCEL VIA WEBSITE

If you signed up on the website, you can cancel your subscription by logging into your account on the SWEET.TV website and following these steps:

  • Navigate to the settings menu in the top right corner of the screen by clicking on your avatar.
  • Choose an option and then select Continue.
  • To finalize the cancellation, select Confirm Cancellation.

CANCEL VIA GOOGLE PLAY STORE

If you subscribed through Google Play, cancel as follows:

  • Open the Google Play Store app.
  • Tap your profile icon.
  • Tap 'Payments & subscriptions', then 'Subscriptions'.
  • Select SWEET.TV to cancel.

CANCEL VIA APPLE APP STORE

If you subscribed through the Apple App Store, cancel as follows:

  • Open the Settings app.
  • Tap your name.
  • Tap 'Subscriptions'.
  • Select SWEET.TV to cancel.
