How to unsubscribe from Apple TV+

Here's how to cancel your Apple TV+ subscription on different devices.

CANCEL APPLE TV+ SUBSCRIPTION ON IOS

To cancel your Apple TV+ subscription on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch, follow these steps:

  • Open the Settings app.
  • Tap your name.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Tap Apple TV+.
  • Tap Cancel Subscription. You might need to scroll down.
  • Confirm the cancellation.
