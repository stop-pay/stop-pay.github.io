How to unsubscribe from Discord Nitro

Learn how to cancel your Discord Nitro or Nitro Basic subscription on desktop, browser, Android, or iOS. You'll retain access to Nitro features until the end of your current billing period.

CANCEL DISCORD NITRO SUBSCRIPTION

  • Go to User Settings by clicking the cogwheel icon next to your Discord username.
  • Navigate to the Subscriptions tab.
  • Click "Cancel" within the Nitro or Nitro Basic banner.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation.
  • Confirm the cancellation.
  • Check your email for a confirmation.
